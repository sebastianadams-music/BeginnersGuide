Journey to Huddersfield 21/11/23


Journey made by Hannah, Jane, Rob, Sebastian, Yseult

We all got a lift with Sebastian's dad David, except for Jane - who was brought by her own dad.

8.05am ferry from Dublin to Holyhead with Irish Ferries

Sebastian misread the schedule and we missed a train at Holyhead, so we stopped for a sausage roll.

We nearly missed the next one by getting on the wrong train (marked 0000), but realised at the last moment...

Train from Holyhead to Crewe, then a change to Manchester Piccadilly.

Then finally, another change to take the train all the way to Huddersfield.

We piled the six of us and all our gear into one taxi, and most of us were shocked to discover that Northern Irish pounds are not legal tender in England.

The travel went very smoothly - but we found the repeated changes of train very tiring. The people who hadn't done SailRail before were amazed at how nice the ferry was, and how chilled out the process was compared to flying.
